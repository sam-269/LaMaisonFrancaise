About Us

La Maison Française is a French term that translates to 'The French house'.

La Maison Française aims to provide an environment of not just French learning classes, but an immersive cultural familiarity of France. This institute aims to promote the French ways of life.

The students at La Maison Française will gain insight into the French lifestyle, enabling them to think like natives and giving them a sense of belongingness. Ultimately, the institute aims to provide high-quality French education within a welcoming and refined atmosphere, ensuring that students feel comfortable, inspired and confident in their journey of French learning.

La Maison Française is ‘Your house of French learning’. We are more than just a place to learn French; we are your home for cultivating a passion for the language and culture.

Our mission is to instil a French learning mindset in the youth, immersing them in the nuances of French culture and fostering a profound understanding of its essence.

Core Values

  • Quality: We are dedicated to delivering the highest standards of education and service. From our curriculum to our facilities, we strive for quality in every aspect of our institution.
  • Excellence: We aim not only to meet but to exceed expectations. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our students receive the best possible learning experience.
  • Learning: At the heart of La Maison Française is a passion for learning. We believe in the transformative power of education and are committed to fostering an environment where students can thrive intellectually, linguistically, and culturally.
  • Commitment: We are deeply committed to the success of our students. Our dedicated teachers and staff go above and beyond to support each individual on their journey to proficiency in the French language and understanding of French culture.
